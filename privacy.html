Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Huon Valley Golf Club collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information in accordance with the Australian **Privacy Act 1988**.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you visit our website, use our Wi-Fi, or make a payment:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Payment Information (for card payments via Square)
  • MAC Address (for Wi-Fi tracking)
  • Voucher Numbers (for cash payments)

Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To process your payments for green fees and equipment hire.
  • To track visitor attendance via MAC addresses.
  • To contact you with relevant information about your visit.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. You may request to have your personal information deleted by contacting us at the details below.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services, such as Square, to process card payments. Your personal information is shared with these services only to the extent necessary for the transaction. Please review their privacy policy for further details.

Security of Your Information

We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of your personal information. This includes using secure encryption for payment processing and secure storage for personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the information provided below.

Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and track MAC addresses of devices connecting to our Wi-Fi for operational purposes, such as ensuring that each visitor pays the required green fees.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, or if you would like to request access to or deletion of your personal information, please contact us:
