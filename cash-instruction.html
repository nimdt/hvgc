Cash Payment Instructions

Follow these steps to complete your cash payment:

  1. Go down the right side of the clubhouse and up the stairs.
  2. Fill in the payment voucher.
  3. Place the money in one of the envelopes supplied.
  4. Write your **name** and **voucher number** on the envelope.
  5. Place the envelope in the payment box.
  6. Place the voucher on your bag for visibility.
  7. Use our Wi-Fi portal to enter your voucher number into the system.

If you have any questions, please reach out to one of our staff members for assistance.
